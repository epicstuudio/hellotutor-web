Getting Started

What is Hello Tutor and how does it work?
Hello Tutor handles everything. You tell us what you need, and we find the right tutor for you, arrange your lessons, and manage payment — all through the platform at app.hellotutor.me. There is no browsing, no negotiating, and no admin on your part. You just show up and learn.
Is Hello Tutor a marketplace where I choose my own tutor?
No. Hello Tutor is not a marketplace. You share your requirements with us, and we find the right tutor, arrange your bookings, and handle payment on your behalf.
Is it free to register?
Yes. Creating an account is free and there is no obligation to book once you have registered. You only pay once you subscribe and confirm your first lesson.
Who can register on the platform?
You must be at least 18 years old to register and book lessons. If the student is under 18, a parent or legal guardian must create the account and provide consent for the child to receive tuition.
Where is Hello Tutor based?
Hello Tutor is owned and operated by Hello Tutor LLC, registered and operating in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. The platform is at app.hellotutor.me. Our Terms and Conditions and Safeguarding Policy are governed by UAE law.

How Matching Works

How does Hello Tutor match me with a tutor?
When you register, we gather information about your learning needs, subject, level, and availability. From there, Hello Tutor takes care of everything — finding the right tutor, arranging your bookings, and handling payment on your behalf. You do not need to negotiate scheduling or rates with anyone.
What if the match does not feel right?
While we take care to match you with the right tutor from the outset, if a match does not work out as expected, please get in touch and we will work to find a tutor who is a stronger fit for your needs. Reach us at support@hellotutor.me.
How are tutors vetted before being approved?
Before any tutor's profile goes live, Hello Tutor carries out a thorough verification process:
  • Identity verification: government-issued photo ID and relevant qualifications are reviewed.
  • Background check: all tutors must hold a valid background check dated within the last three years. Tutors whose checks lapse are suspended until the requirement is met.
  • Written references: two written references from non-family members are required.
  • UAE work or freelance permit: non-UAE national tutors must hold a valid MOHRE permit or equivalent free zone authority permit.
If you have a concern about a tutor, contact us before proceeding with a lesson.
Are tutors employed by Hello Tutor?
No. Tutors on Hello Tutor are independent contractors, not Hello Tutor employees. When you confirm your first booking, you enter into a direct contract with your tutor for the delivery of lessons. Hello Tutor facilitates the match and provides the booking and payment infrastructure.
Should I also verify my tutor's credentials myself?
While Hello Tutor carries out checks on all tutors before approving them, you are also responsible for using your own judgement about a tutor's suitability. We recommend verifying any qualifications or experience claims before or at the start of your first lesson.

Lessons and Bookings

How do I confirm my first booking?
Once Hello Tutor has matched you with a tutor, you will confirm your first booking through the platform. You must have valid payment details saved on your account to do so.
What happens after I confirm my first booking?
By confirming your first booking, you authorise Hello Tutor to arrange further bookings on your behalf automatically and to take payment for each lesson. You will be notified of every booking by email and through the platform. Please always check that bookings are correct.
Do I arrange lessons directly with my tutor?
No. All bookings are arranged by Hello Tutor on your behalf. You must not contact tutors to arrange lessons outside the platform, and all communication must go through Hello Tutor's messaging system.
What do I need to have ready before each lesson?
Before each lesson, make sure you have shared any relevant learning objectives with Hello Tutor, your device, webcam, microphone, and internet connection are working, your payment details are up to date, and your lesson environment is appropriate and free from distraction.
Are lessons recorded?
Yes. All lessons are recorded by Hello Tutor for safeguarding and quality purposes. Recordings are stored for a minimum of 28 days and a maximum of one year, and remain Hello Tutor's property. They may be reviewed internally, shared with UAE law enforcement if required, or shared with students where appropriate.

Students must not independently record lessons by any means outside Hello Tutor's own recording system.

What if my tutor does not show up?
If your tutor does not join the Online Classroom within the first 15 minutes of the scheduled start time, you will not be charged. If your tutor cancels a confirmed booking, you will receive a full refund. Please raise a complaint within 96 hours of the scheduled lesson end time.
What if I miss my lesson?
If you do not attend a lesson, the full lesson fee will still be charged. Bookings cannot be cancelled once a lesson has started. If you are unable to attend due to exceptional circumstances, contact us and a refund may be considered at Hello Tutor's sole discretion.
Can my tutor complete my coursework or essays for me?
No. Tutors are strictly prohibited from completing or intending to complete coursework, essays, assignments, or similar academic work on a student's behalf. Any tutor found in breach of this may be removed from the platform.
Can I leave a review for my tutor?
Yes. After your lessons you can leave a review, comment, or rating for your tutor through the platform. Reviews are from verified students only, help maintain quality across the platform, and contribute to how tutors are ranked in our system.

Subscriptions and Payment

How is pricing structured?
Hello Tutor operates on a subscription model. Your lesson fee is determined by the subject, education level, and number of lessons included in your subscription. All fees are displayed before you subscribe and include any applicable UAE taxes.
What does the lesson fee include?
Your lesson fee is made up of the Tutor Fee (the portion paid to your tutor) and Hello Tutor's Service Fee (which covers matching, booking, and platform services). The Service Fee is included within the lesson fee and is not an additional charge.
When am I charged for a lesson?
Payment is taken at the point of subscribing. All fees are displayed clearly before you confirm your subscription.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept major debit and credit cards. Payments are processed securely by our third-party payment provider. Hello Tutor does not store your full card details on its own servers.
What happens if a payment fails?
A failed payment occurs when our payment provider cannot charge your registered card.
  • 48 and 72 hours after the lesson: payment is automatically retried.
  • 96 hours after the lesson: all future bookings are cancelled until the outstanding amount is cleared.
Hello Tutor may pursue outstanding amounts for up to 90 days and reserves the right to refer unpaid debts to a third-party collection service, which may affect your credit record.
Does Hello Tutor have a referral programme?
Yes. If you refer a friend, you will earn platform credit once they attend their first paid lesson. Credit is valid for 3 months from the date of award, cannot be exchanged for cash, and is only earned for paid lessons. Hello Tutor may cancel credit if fraudulent activity is suspected.

Cancellations and Refunds

Can I cancel a lesson?
You can cancel a booking at any point before the lesson starts, but cancellation charges apply:
  • More than 24 hours before the lesson: 50% of the lesson fee is charged.
  • Within 24 hours of the lesson: 100% of the lesson fee is charged.
Cancellations must be made through the platform. Once a lesson has started it cannot be cancelled.
Missed lessons: if you do not attend without cancelling beforehand, the full lesson fee is charged.
What if my tutor cancels?
On rare occasions, a tutor may need to cancel due to unforeseen circumstances. If this happens, Hello Tutor will be in touch to reschedule your lesson as soon as possible. If rescheduling is not possible, you will receive a full refund.
How do I raise a complaint about a lesson?
A complaint covers situations where the tutor's service fell below the standard you reasonably expected, or where your lesson did not take place as booked.
  • Contact Hello Tutor by chat or email within 96 hours of the scheduled lesson end time.
  • We will contact your tutor within 72 hours of receiving your complaint.
  • If the tutor does not dispute the complaint within 48 hours of being notified, a full refund of the tutor fee will be processed.
Complaints raised after 96 hours will still be reviewed, but the outcome is at Hello Tutor's discretion. Email us at [email protected].
Can I get a refund if I was unhappy with lesson quality?
Yes. If you believe the quality of your lesson fell below a reasonable standard, raise a complaint within 96 hours of the lesson ending. Hello Tutor will review the situation and process a refund where appropriate.

Online Classroom

What is the Hello Tutor Online Classroom?
The Online Classroom is Hello Tutor's own built-in lesson space, available directly through the platform at app.hellotutor.me. All lessons take place here.
What equipment do I need?
You will need a laptop, tablet, or desktop computer (recommended over mobile), a stable internet connection, and a working webcam and microphone. It is your responsibility to ensure everything is working before your lesson. No refund will be given if you cannot access the Online Classroom due to issues within your own control.
What if there is a technical problem during my lesson?
If the issue is caused by something within your control, such as your internet connection or device, no refund will be given. If Hello Tutor's platform itself fails and this prevents a lesson from taking place, Hello Tutor will refund the Service Fee for that lesson. In either case, please report the issue to us at [email protected].

Safety and Safeguarding

How does Hello Tutor keep students safe?
The safety and wellbeing of all students, especially children, is our highest priority. Our safeguarding measures include:
  • Identity verification: government-issued photo ID and qualifications reviewed for every tutor.
  • Background checks: mandatory for all tutors, valid within the last three years.
  • Written references: two non-family references required for all tutors.
  • Lesson recording: all lessons are recorded and retained for safeguarding review.
  • Platform monitoring: messages and activity are monitored to prevent misuse.
  • Designated Safeguarding Officer: a named officer handles all concerns raised through the platform.
Hello Tutor cooperates fully with UAE law enforcement in connection with any safeguarding concern.
My child is under 18. What do I need to know?
A parent or legal guardian must register the account and provide consent for any student under 18. As the responsible adult, you must not leave a child in the unsupervised sole care of a tutor during a lesson, must remain available so your child can report concerns without delay, and must ensure the lesson environment is appropriate and free from risk. By booking, you consent on the student's behalf to their lessons being recorded for safeguarding and quality purposes.
Can a parent or guardian sit in on a lesson?
Yes. Parents are welcome to observe any lesson. Please let us know in advance so the tutor can plan accordingly.
Can a tutor cancel a lesson if no parent is present?
Yes. Where a student is under 18 and no parent or legal guardian is available, a tutor may cancel the lesson if either the tutor or the student does not feel comfortable continuing. We strongly recommend that a parent or guardian is always reachable during lessons involving students under 18.
What happens when a safeguarding concern is reported?
Any user reported for a safeguarding breach is immediately suspended pending investigation. Where suspected illegal activity is involved, the user may be permanently barred and reported to UAE law enforcement. Where a concern relates to a child, Hello Tutor will always prioritise that child's immediate safety and welfare above all other considerations.
How do I report a safeguarding concern?
Contact Hello Tutor immediately at [email protected] if you have any concern about the safety or welfare of a student, or if you encounter anything inappropriate or illegal on the platform. All reports are handled by our Safeguarding Officer and treated as a priority.
What should I do if a tutor contacts me outside the platform?
All communication between students and tutors must take place within Hello Tutor's platform. Tutors are not permitted to contact students or parents using personal details obtained through the platform. If you receive any unsolicited contact from a tutor outside the platform, report it immediately at [email protected].
How is my personal data protected?
Hello Tutor protects your personal information in accordance with our Privacy Policy and UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on the Protection of Personal Data. For privacy-related queries or to exercise your data rights, contact [email protected].

What am I responsible for in terms of content and communications?
You are solely responsible for all content you post or send on the platform. You must not share anything that is defamatory, offensive, misleading, illegal, or that infringes a third party's intellectual property rights, and must not send unsolicited commercial messages to other users.

If you encounter any inappropriate or illegal content from another user, report it immediately at [email protected].

Joining Hello Tutor

How do I join Hello Tutor as a tutor?
You apply through the tutor onboarding process at app.hellotutor.me. Hello Tutor will review your application, verify your identity and credentials, and carry out the required checks before deciding whether to approve your profile. Your profile only goes live once Hello Tutor determines it is sufficiently complete and verified.
What checks are required before I can start tutoring?
Before your profile goes live, you must provide:
  • Government-issued identity document and evidence of relevant qualifications.
  • A valid background check dated within the last three years. If your check lapses while you are a registered tutor, your profile will be suspended immediately until you provide an updated one.
  • Two written references from individuals who are not family members, confirming their relationship to you and consenting to the reference being shared.
  • UAE work or freelance permit (non-UAE nationals only): a valid permit from MOHRE or an equivalent free zone authority. Failure to hold or maintain this permit will result in immediate suspension of your profile.
You must also disclose any criminal convictions or cautions at registration and notify Hello Tutor of any received afterwards for as long as you remain a registered tutor.

No. You are an independent contractor, not a Hello Tutor employee. You are solely responsible for your own tax obligations, professional insurance, and UAE permits. When a student confirms their first booking with you, they enter into a direct contract with you for the delivery of lessons.
Do I need to keep my profile information up to date?
Yes. All information on your profile — including qualifications, subjects, rates, and personal statement — must be accurate, complete, and not misleading. Update this information promptly whenever it changes. Hello Tutor may suspend or remove profiles that contain inaccurate or misleading information.
How am I ranked on the platform?
Tutor profiles are ranked based on profile completeness, student ratings, and number of lessons taught. Positive reviews, prompt responses to communications, and a higher volume of lessons delivered all contribute to a better ranking. Hello Tutor reserves the right to change the ranking methodology at any time.

Safeguarding and Your Account

What are my safeguarding duties?
You have a duty to protect the safety and welfare of all students, particularly those under 18. Your safeguarding responsibilities include:
  • Disclosing any criminal convictions or cautions at registration and at any point afterwards.
  • Disclosing any other relevant information that may lead Hello Tutor to determine you are not suitable to provide tutoring services.
  • Reporting immediately to Hello Tutor any concerns you have about a student's welfare or safety.
  • Ensuring all contact with students is appropriate to your role and confined to the subject area.
  • Reporting any inappropriate, offensive, or illegal material you encounter on the platform.
How do I report a safeguarding concern?
Contact Hello Tutor immediately at [email protected] if you have any concern about the safety or welfare of a student, or if you encounter suspected illegal activity on the platform. All safeguarding reports are handled by our designated Safeguarding Officer and treated as a priority.
What happens if a complaint or safeguarding concern is raised about me?
If a complaint is raised, Hello Tutor will notify you within 72 hours of receiving it. You will have 48 hours to dispute it. If you do not respond within that time, Hello Tutor may process a full refund of the tutor fee to the student.

If a safeguarding concern or suspected illegal activity is reported, you will be immediately suspended from the platform pending investigation. Where illegal activity is involved, you may be permanently barred and reported to UAE law enforcement. Hello Tutor will cooperate fully with any official investigation, including providing access to lesson recordings.

Do I need to renew my background check?
Yes. Your background check must have been issued within the last three years at all times. If it expires, your profile will be suspended immediately until you provide an updated one. It is your responsibility to renew before it lapses. Hello Tutor may request evidence of your background check status at any time.
